{{ page.origTitles[0] }}

A
spreadsheet
for desertification.

An
uncontrollable process
processed through,
Like a cliff face which just now buckled.

But always unfinished!

{{ page.origTitles[1] }}

     Even the green ones

Wilt some.

     Their

Suckers

     Don’t speak

They mouth.

     They eye.

Out of what vengeance?

     Secret,

Silent,

     Glass bloom—

Rounder,

     Down into itself

Drops:

     Sphinx.
